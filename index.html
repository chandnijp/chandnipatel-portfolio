ABOUT ME

I am an up and coming web developer, based in London.

With a background heavily focused in both Art and Maths, I have naturally found myself in the development world. I enjoy the creative, imaginative aspect of front-end development as well as the complex, problem-solving side to the back-end.

I am curious by nature and enjoy figuring out how things work. I am also a perfectionist; attention to detail is key and I like to play around with colour; building websites that are simple and clean, yet attractive.

Having built web applications using a number of languages and completing Colt Steel's Web Development Bootcamp and Andrei's Zero to Mastery course, I have a strong foundation in web design and development.
